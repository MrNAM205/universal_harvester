# OmniVeroBrix Autonomy Stress Test Plan (v1.0)

This is the highest-intensity validation protocol targeting the Autonomy Loop to ensure it remains bounded, safe, coherent, and predictable under maximum load.

## 1. Phase-Cycle Acceleration Stress
- **Objective:** Force rapid cycling through Plan -> Act -> Observe -> Reflect -> Decide.
- **Procedure:** Run on 20-50 missions, force immediate observations, accelerate cycles.
- **Expected:** Bounded cycles, no runaway loops, no skipped phases.

## 2. Multi-Mission Autonomy Stress
- **Objective:** Test coordination across many missions at once.
- **Procedure:** Enable autonomy for 100+ missions, force cross-mission sharing.
- **Expected:** Global coherence, no starvation, no deadlocks.

## 3. Contradictory Intelligence Stress
- **Objective:** Test handling of conflicting/ambiguous information.
- **Procedure:** Ingest conflicting dates, issuers, and procedural rules.
- **Expected:** Identifies contradictions, stable reflections, conservative decisions.

## 4. Dependency-Chain Stress
- **Objective:** Test navigation of long dependency chains.
- **Procedure:** Create 10-20 step dependencies, introduce missing inputs mid-cycle.
- **Expected:** Resolves correctly, no infinite loops, no dependency collapse.

## 5. Temporal Compression Stress
- **Objective:** Test reasoning under extreme timeline density.
- **Procedure:** Ingest dozens of deadlines, force timeline alignment.
- **Expected:** Temporal coherence, no collapse, no deadlocks.

## 6. Reflection-Density Stress
- **Objective:** Overload reflective reasoning.
- **Procedure:** Generate 100+ reflections, introduce ambiguous observations.
- **Expected:** Coherent reflections, no runaway behavior.

## 7. Decision-Pressure Stress
- **Objective:** Test decision making under extreme pressure.
- **Procedure:** Time-sensitive missions, conflicting priorities.
- **Expected:** Bounded decisions, no unsafe actions, no paralysis.

## Final Stress Test: Sovereign Autonomy Overload Mission
Run a dedicated audit mission using the entire mission universe to produce a comprehensive stability and safety report.
