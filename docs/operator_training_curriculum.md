# OmniVeroBrix Operator Training Curriculum (v1.0)

This curriculum is the structured learning pathway for a new operator, teaching them how to orchestrate missions, supervise autonomy, validate evidence, interpret remittance, align corpus intelligence, and maintain system health.

## 1. Module 1 — Cockpit Orientation & Operator Role
- **Objective:** Build foundational understanding.
- **Topics:** Architecture, responsibilities, navigation, mission lifecycle, autonomy loop.

## 2. Module 2 — Intake & Ingestion
- **Objective:** Train operators to bring documents into the system correctly.
- **Topics:** Procurement, ingestion pipeline, OCR validation, entity/remittance detection.

## 3. Module 3 — Evidence & Document Analysis
- **Objective:** Teach interpretation of harvested documents.
- **Topics:** Metadata validation, entity normalization, timeline extraction, document linking.

## 4. Module 4 — Corpus Intelligence
- **Objective:** Align documents with statutes/regulations.
- **Topics:** Corpus navigation, citation search, procedural mapping, cross-referencing.

## 5. Module 5 — Remittance Intelligence
- **Objective:** Interpret financial instruments.
- **Topics:** Detection, coupon parsing, barcode interpretation, issuer/account clustering.

## 6. Module 6 — Mission Creation
- **Objective:** Create structured missions.
- **Topics:** Mission types, inputs, plan preview, subsystem routing, dependency detection.

## 7. Module 7 — Mission Execution & Autonomy Oversight
- **Objective:** Supervise execution and autonomy cycles.
- **Topics:** Step execution, subsystem logs, autonomy phases, reflections, decisions, stop conditions.

## 8. Module 8 — System Health & Safety
- **Objective:** Maintain system stability.
- **Topics:** Subsystem health, resource monitoring, alerts, restart procedures, memory stability.

## Certification Pathway
To become a Certified OmniVeroBrix Operator, the trainee must complete all modules and pass the associated hands-on exercises in the cockpit.
