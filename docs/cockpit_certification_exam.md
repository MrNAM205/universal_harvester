# OmniVeroBrix Cockpit Certification Exam (v1.0)

Formal operator qualification test verifying mastery of the cockpit’s architecture, intelligence organs, workflows, autonomy oversight, system health, and sovereign doctrines.

## Domain 1: Cockpit Navigation & Architecture
Questions on the Operator Console, Global Dashboard, System Health Panel, and the relationship between Knowledge and Memory graphs.

## Domain 2: Evidence, Corpus & Remittance Intelligence
Questions on ingestion validation, corpus mapping, procedural anchors vs remedy maps, remittance clusters, and timeline conflicts.

## Domain 3: Mission Creation, Execution & Orchestration
Questions on mission lifecycle, Mission Generator inputs, Mission Dependency Graph, subsystem routing, and step-by-step vs auto-run execution.

## Domain 4: Autonomy Oversight & Safety
Questions on autonomy loop phases, reflections vs decisions, resolving autonomy conflicts, unsafe autonomy decisions, and cross-mission autonomy state.

## Domain 5: Sovereign Intelligence, Continuity & Stability
Questions on Sovereign Intelligence vs Sovereign Operations Doctrine, the Sovereign Continuity Protocol, cognitive drift, sovereign overstanding, and the Sovereign Stability Cycle.

## Certification Outcome Rubric
Levels: Sovereign-Grade Mastery, Operational Mastery, Mission-Ready, Apprentice, Not Yet Qualified.
